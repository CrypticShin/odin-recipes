Eggs on a Grill

Description

It couldn't be easier to make this sandwich with just bread, eggs, butter, and Cheddar cheese. The simple breakfast comes together in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • Bacon
  • Canola oil
  • Refrigerator crescent rolls

Steps

  • Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat.
  • Coat all holes of a muffin pan with cooking spray and crack an egg into each hole.
  • Place on grill and grill over medium high heat for 2 minutes, or to desired doneness.
